# Russo's Family Kitchen — AI Automation Plan

**Business:** Russo's Family Kitchen · Scranton, PA
**Industry:** Restaurant (Italian-American)

---

## The Problem

Russo's is a busy family-owned restaurant. During the dinner rush (5–8pm), staff can't answer the phone. Catering and private event inquiries come in but nobody follows up consistently. Satisfied customers leave without being asked for a review. And when the kitchen closes, the business goes dark — no one is answering questions or capturing leads.

Every one of these gaps is a missed revenue opportunity.

---

## Recommended Automations

### 1. Missed Call Text-Back
**What it does:** When any call goes unanswered, an SMS is automatically sent within 30 seconds.

> *"Hi! This is Russo's Family Kitchen — sorry we missed you. How can we help? Reply here and we'll get back to you right away."*

**Why it matters:** Most people won't leave a voicemail, but they will reply to a text. This recovers 20–30% of calls that would otherwise be lost forever.

---

### 2. Catering Inquiry Follow-Up Sequence
**What it does:** When someone submits a catering inquiry (via chatbot, website, or phone), a 3-touch sequence fires automatically:
- Touch 1 (same day): "Thanks for reaching out! We received your catering inquiry and will be in touch shortly."
- Touch 2 (next morning): Personalized follow-up with menu PDF and direct scheduling link.
- Touch 3 (3 days later, if no reply): Gentle nudge — "Don't let your event planning slip — we'd love to help."

**Why it matters:** Catering events are $500–$3,000+ tickets. Losing one because nobody followed up is expensive. This sequence closes that gap completely.

---

### 3. Post-Visit Review Request
**What it does:** 2 hours after a reservation ends, an SMS goes out:

> *"Thanks for dining with us tonight! If you enjoyed your meal, we'd love a quick Google review — it means everything to a family-owned restaurant. [LINK]"*

**Why it matters:** Google reviews directly affect how many new customers find the restaurant. 4–8 automated review requests per month compounds over time into a significant competitive advantage.

---

### 4. Reservation Reminders
**What it does:** Automated SMS reminders are sent 24 hours and 2 hours before every reservation, giving guests an easy way to confirm or cancel.

**Why it matters:** No-shows cost restaurants real money in wasted prep, staffing, and held tables. A 30–40% reduction in no-shows translates directly to the bottom line.

---

### 5. Lead → CRM Sync + Daily Digest
**What it does:** Every new contact captured (chatbot, missed call, web form) is automatically logged to a Google Sheet. The owner receives a daily 8am email digest of new leads.

**Why it matters:** Right now leads likely live in voicemails, sticky notes, and someone's memory. This creates a real, searchable pipeline with zero manual work.
